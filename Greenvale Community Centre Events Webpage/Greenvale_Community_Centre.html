Community Clean-up Day

On June 8th, 2025 join the community to walk around the neighbourhood and help clean and beautify the playgrounds, parks, and the area in front on the retention pond. Tools will be provided such as gloves, shovels, garbage bags etc., to assist with the initiative. We will be meeting at the community center for 9:00am. This initiative follows the #WeCleanTT drive, where the Ministry of Rural Development and Local Governement and CEPEP conducts a clean-up drive in prepardness for the rainy season. As a community, we are responsible to take care of our environs and ensure that our drainage systems are clear of debris. Learn more about the Ministry of Rural Development and Local Government rainy season preparedness campaign at #WeCleanTT Initiative
